Reuse Reduce Recycle

Assignment Prompt: “Reduce, Reuse, and Recycle,” has been a motto of the environmental movement in the United States since the 1970s, intended to reduce the amount of waste produced by consumers. As we’ve moved into the Information Age, we have developed new forms of waste, like electronic waste and “digital exhaust,” that are increasingly problematic (we will read some texts on this). I challenge you in this class to try to incorporate the environmental ethos of reusing and recycling into your projects.

Look through the RISD Nature Lab Visual Collection on Flickr (Links to an external site.), and select one image. Consider textures, colors, shapes, or subjects you could imagine using in multiple ways. For Project 1, Project 2, and the Final Project in this class, find some way to incorporate the image or aspects of the image.
